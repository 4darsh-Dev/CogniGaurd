What Dark Patterns are and How these affect you?

Tricky Navigation: Misleading users into clicking on things they didnot intend to.

Hidden Data Collection: Gathering user data without clear permission or making it hard to opt out.

Bait and Switch: Promising attractive offers that change or vanish upon closer look.

Fake Urgency: Creating a false sense of urgency or scarcity, like fake countdown timers or "limited-time" offers.

Difficult Cancellations: Making it hard to cancel subscriptions with confusing steps or hidden fees.
